EasySort:

Maintaining and developing EasySort, an open-source end-to-end approach to sorting trash by use of robots.

EasySort may not survive enough to see the end, but that is exactly the point behind it. Anyone can continue our work, modify and improve it or even contribute to improving our approach.

Sorting trash is not a unique problem: it is most likely something every country has to go through. Instead of having 100 companies trying to beat each other, why not together create the solution 3-5x faster, with the only downside being that everyone wins?
