Why EasySort?
Many communities will in a short time be in a position, where they needed to effectively sort trash in order to take of not only the global but also local environment. Continuing to rely on oil to produce new plastics is not a reliable option. There are two ways to fix this issue: 1) Develop new renewable methods that can substitute oil. Probably not only substitute but significantly improve the process in some way. Or 2) Find a way to effectively sort trash to be reused without having to decrease the quality.
